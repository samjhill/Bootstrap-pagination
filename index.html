Bootstrap-pagination

by Sam Hill

Bootstrap-pagination is a fork of this jQuery paging plugin. Its purpose is to create a pagination plugin that required no CSS to function. However, for those who like Bootstrap in their projects, but still need automatic pagination, I wanted to up the aesthetics a bit. Here is the result.
